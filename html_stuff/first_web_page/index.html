Odin

Odin is the chief of the Aesir gods. He is the god of wisdom, poetry, and war. He is the husband of Frigg, the father of Thor, and the grandfather of Magni and Modi.

Thor

Thor is the god of thunder and lightning. He is the son of Odin and Jord, the earth goddess. He is the strongest of the Aesir, and is the defender of the gods and their fortress, Asgard.
